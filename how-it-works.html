How It Works

A step-by-step guide to our phone-based cancellation assistance service

Step by Step

Our Process

01

Choose Your Provider

Select Verizon, Spectrum, Optimum, or AT&T. Review service details and pricing before proceeding.

02

Call Our Support Line

Speak with our team. We'll guide you through what to expect and help prepare your account information.

03

Complete Cancellation

We assist you through the call with scripts and a checklist to confirm equipment returns, final bill, etc.

What to Have Ready

Before calling us, gather this information if possible:

  • Account number - Found on your monthly bill
  • Name on account - The registered account holder
  • Service address - Where you receive service
  • Verification info - PIN, security questions, or last 4 of SSN
  • Preferred end date - When you want service to stop

Expected Timing

  • Our consultation call: 15-25 minutes
  • Your call to provider: 20-60 minutes (varies)
  • Service disconnection: Usually 1-14 days
  • Equipment return deadline: Typically 7-21 days

What "Successful Cancellation" Means

With our assistance, you will:

  • Understand the cancellation process for your provider
  • Be prepared with the right information and talking points
  • Know what to expect regarding equipment returns and final bills
  • Have a checklist to confirm all steps during your provider call
